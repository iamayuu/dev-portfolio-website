Project Overview

I worked as the Automation Developer and Technical Lead for the client where I designed, built and scaled RPA solutions using the iBot automation platform. The engagement involved delivering two major processes which together operated through a network of 23 bots. These processes formed part of a larger automation program with 108 bots deployed across the client landscape.

My role required leading API integration work, designing token based authentication flows for secure bot communication and troubleshooting complex issues in a high dependency environment. I was also responsible for guiding developers, reviewing bot architecture and ensuring that our implementations met performance, reliability and governance standards.

During this project I became the primary technical point of contact for new enhancements and critical fixes. I handled client communication, analysed requirements and solved issues that required deep technical understanding across APIs, RPA logic and system interactions.

Key Contributions
