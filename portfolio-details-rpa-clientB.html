Project Overview

Automated a high volume customer servicing enquiry process in which Customers often request details about their annual service visit, repair activity or appliance checks. Before automation, service agents manually reviewed each request, searched through multiple systems and consolidated the findings into an email, fax or letter based on the customer's preference.

The automation replaced this entire workflow using Automation Anywhere's intelligent automation tools. The bot analysed the incoming customer request using AI classification to determine what information the customer needed, extracted relevant service and appliance data from five to six internal applications and generated a personalised response using sentiment analysis to ensure the customer received a contextual, human-like reply instead of a generic template.

The end-to-end automation significantly reduced manual load, improved turnaround time and ensured consistent and high-quality communication for all servicing enquiries.

Key Contributions

  • Automated customer Annual Servicing Enquiry process handling requests for appliance repair and servicing details.
  • Used Automation Anywhere's AI modules to classify customer intent and understand what information was required.
  • Integrated with 5 to 6 internal applications to fetch service history, appliance details, engineer notes and related data.
  • Implemented sentiment-based response generation to create personalised letters, emails or fax outputs based on customer tone.
